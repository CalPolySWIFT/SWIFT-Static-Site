What is SWIFT?

We are Students with an Interest in the Future of Technology, we are t he premier information technology and security organization at Cal Poly Pomona.

Our mission is simple, for over 23 years we aim to inspire and create a generation of students who are aware and capable of dealing with the dynamic landscape of information security, cyber security, and information technology.

Together we can build a secure future through training, experience, and education. Let's work together to create and learn something new.

Don't just take our word for it. Whether you are an industry professional or a curious student, take a look at our previous events. While you're at it, come to one of our unique workshops, trainings, or conferences and see what makes us SWIFT.

Meetings

Ready to be inspired? Come to our weekly meetings where we will introduce you to the latest technology from peers and professionals. With topics focusing on anything from security, networking, automation, or car hacking we bring you inspiring topics that will allow you to are that interest you most.

Events

Whether you're learning the basics of Linux, or recovering artifacts from network forensics, or perhaps just want to relax and game, we provide you basic, and advanced, training, workshops, and activities that will help you not only get an edge in security but build friendships that last far beyond Cal Poly.

Competitions

Ready to take your skills to the next level? There is no better way to learn than focusing on challenges that put you up against the best in the field. Apply your skills building defensive networks, or be offensive and attaack them. It's up to you, come have fun and learn!

Membership

If you like solving problems, breaking things, being with fellow hackers, you definitely want to get involved. Our membership provides you with resources to upgrade your college experience, and your professional outlook. Join today! Or find out more!
