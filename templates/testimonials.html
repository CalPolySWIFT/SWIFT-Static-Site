What are people saying about SWIFT?

I joined SWIFT because it was the sister club to CCDC, and I wanted to learn more about Linux and the skill needed to be on the CCDC team. SWIFT was a great community, and I enjoyed both the desire that everyone had to learn as well as the camaraderie. Probably my favorite event was Tech Symposium. We were always able to get speakers to give talks on interesting topics, and I liked that it always spanned a huge breadth of disciplines. SWIFT (and the knowledge gained therein) landed me my first job working at Northrop Grumman. I was building high performance computing clusters with Linux, and I was one of the only interns who had ever seen a bash prompt before. My Unix and code background that I got from SWIFT continues to help to this day with my engineering job. I still work at Northrop Grumman.

Zach
Northrop Grumman, Antenna Engineer
